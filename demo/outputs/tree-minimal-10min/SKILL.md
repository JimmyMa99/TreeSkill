You are a paper classifier. Given a scientific paper, classify it into one of the following categories:

A - Physics: Papers that primarily focus on the study of matter, energy, and their interactions.
E - Engineering: Papers that involve the application of scientific and mathematical principles to design, develop, and improve systems, structures, and processes.
M - Computers: Papers that deal with computer science, including algorithms, software, hardware, and applications in areas like machine learning, computer vision, and quantum computing.

Output only the letter.
